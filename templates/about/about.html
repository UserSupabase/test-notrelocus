About us

Welcome to Notre Locus, a unique establishment that embodies the essence of a bookshop, art gallery, and event space, all rolled into one. At our core, we strive to foster a profound sense of connection and promote cultural diversity.

A Home Away From Home: Notre Locus - Where Connection and Culture Converge

Unveiling Cultural Kaleidoscope: Notre Locus - Where Art, Literature, and Community Converge

Our primary mission is to offer a space that feels like home, regardless of how far away your actual home might be. We understand the challenges and joys of living abroad, as we were founded by expats, just like you. Whether you're a seasoned globetrotter or a newcomer to the expat community, Notre Locus is your "home from home."

As a bookshop, we curate a diverse collection of literature from all corners of the world. We believe that books hold the power to transport readers to far-off places, introducing them to new cultures and perspectives.

Our shelves are filled with literary treasures, awaiting discovery by avid readers and curious souls alike.

Beyond the literary wonders, Notre Locus proudly serves as an art gallery, showcasing the talent and creativity of local and international artists. Our walls come alive with captivating paintings, thought-provoking sculptures, and awe-inspiring photographs, creating an immersive experience that celebrates art's ability to bridge gaps and foster understanding.

Furthermore, we are not just a place for art and literature appreciation; we are a vibrant hub for cultural events and gatherings. From engaging workshops and discussions to exciting performances and film screenings, Notre Locus becomes a bustling center of cultural exchange and enrichment. Our calendar is always brimming with activities that aim to bring people together and create meaningful connections.
